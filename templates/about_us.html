WHAT IS PAT





Welcome to PAT-PET ACCESSORIES ATELIER, your premier destination for all things pets! Nestled in the heart of TBILISI , we are dedicated to providing a haven for animal lovers and their beloved companions. At PAT, we understand the unique bond between humans and animals. Our mission is to enrich the lives of pets and their owners by offering a wide range of high-quality products and personalized services tailored to meet every need.

Founded 2024, our journey began with a simple yet powerful vision: to create a community hub where pets are celebrated, cherished, and cared for with utmost love and attention. We have grown into a trusted resource for pet owners seeking not only supplies but also expert advice and compassionate care. What sets us apart is our commitment to excellence. Whether you're looking for premium pet food, stylish accessories, or professional grooming services, our knowledgeable team is here to assist you every step of the way. We take pride in maintaining a warm, welcoming atmosphere where pets feel at home and customers become family.

Step into PAT and discover a paradise of pet accessories that combine practicality with flair. Our shelves are adorned with a curated selection of premium products, from cozy beds and stylish collars to durable leashes and fashionable apparel. Whether your furry friend prefers chic simplicity or bold designs, PAT has something to suit every taste and personality.

Our knowledgeable staff are passionate pet enthusiasts who are eager to assist you in finding the perfect accessories for your pet. Whether you're searching for the ideal toy to keep them entertained or a practical solution to enhance their comfort, we're here to provide expert guidance and personalized recommendations.

At PAT, we understand that every pet deserves the best. Looking to pamper your pet even more? Explore our range of luxurious grooming essentials and wellness products designed to keep your pet looking and feeling their best. Whether it's a soothing shampoo, a stylish bow tie, or a cozy blanket, PAT has everything you need to indulge your pet in comfort and style.
